Clear Browser Cache

To see the new Settings tab with AI controls, you need to clear your browser cache. Click the button below to automatically clear cache and reload.


Manual Cache Clear Instructions

  1. Windows/Linux: Press Ctrl + Shift + Delete
  2. Mac: Press Cmd + Shift + Delete
  3. Select "Cached images and files"
  4. Click "Clear data"
  5. Close and reopen the browser tab
